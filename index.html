Create a Project Plan fast

Enter Key Tasks, create your Teams and invite your Guests in less than 10 minutes.

Road Map

Plan the big picture for every Project with the Gantt table Road Map. Easily adjust dates or Task order, assign tasks to Team members, create dependencies, add Milestones / Sprints and check progress for each Task in your Projects.

Advanced Task Management

Organise Tasks into clear sections and divide them up into Sub Tasks and To Do Lists making complicated issues easier to complete.

Clear Roles and Responsibilities

Assign each part of your Tasks a clear owner so everyone knows who is responsible, what they need to do and how long they've got to do it.

Real time Messenger

Communicate with colleagues within the app through private message or Team Group Chat. Send attachments and emoji's - it's just like using your phone.

Request Feedback

Request direct feedback from one or more colleagues about specific aspects inside a task and keep all correspondence linked directly to that specific issue. You can even add attachments or provide links.

Assign Followers

Add teammates as followers to a Task so they can stay in the loop, receive relevant task updates and contribute to specific discussions.

Relevant Communication

Customize the information you want to receive and stay updated with notifications and updates to cut back on meetings and micromanaging.

Multiple Work Views

Work the way that suits you best with Task Lists, To Do Lists, Kanban Boards and Project Calendars to organize your Tasks and manage your Projects. (Image: Gif moving through the Task Lists tab headings).

Dependencies

Prevent costly mistakes with Task dependencies that make it clear which Tasks are ready to start and which tasks need to be completed before moving on to the next one. (Picture or Gif: Assigning a dependency in the Road Map).

Task Status and Progress

Keep your workflow moving with status updates on your Tasks and track Task progress to completion. (Image: Task Lists Results Table).

Filter Management

Focus on the exact data you need to make decisions and complete your work.

Plan My Work

Each team member can plan their working day, deal with overdue items and clearly prioritise what tasks need resolved first.

Updates

Get real time automatic updates about the projects, conversations, and tasks that matter to you.

Attachments

Add files from your computer, Dropbox, Box or Google Drive to any Task or Chat. Then view and filter your files by individual Task, Project level or even overall Program level.

Dashboard

Stay informed with a snapshot view of important upcoming Events, Milestones, Sprints,Tasks and the Status and Workload of each Project.

Privacy Control

Limit access to any Project, create hidden teams for sensitive work or make public documents and manuals so your entire organization can access them. (image of making a document public or private)

User Access

Set the parameters and permissions of your users and decide which parts of your business to keep private and what to share.(Image: all the sections with each users name and tick boxes).

File Storage

Keep all your important files in one place, add updated versions and access them from any device.Just like a portable filing cabinet.

Resource Planning

Manage your businesses resources (be they people and time or equipment, material or finances) effectively where and when they are needed. (Gif of booking a meeting room on your calendar or booking out some vans from your company carpool in the resources section).

Ready Made Solutions

As your business grows combine our other modules to deal with new challenges (CRM 25: Marketing 25, Sales 25, Finance 25 and HR 25) everything you need in one easy program.

Desktop and Mobile App

Run your business from any device and work from anywhere. (Images of Desktop, tablet, mobile with the same page).

Keeping Records

Store all your past information details in Project Portfolio's (entire Projects, client details, invoices) so its always accessible when you might need it for future work.

Templates

Re-use successful Project templates to increase efficiency, save time and money and provide consistency for your teams.

Create Estimated and Real Budgets

Create an Estimate to share with your clients for approval or for internal use. As work progresses create your real budget and monitor both in real time to keep your budgets on track. (An estimated budget template picture or both est. and real in comparison table).

Apply your own currency

Users world wide can prepare Invoices using their own currencies (an image of currency icons going everywhere).

Send and Track Invoices

Record and keep track of your Invoices as you send them and update them when you are paid.

Customize your Invoices

Better reflect your brand by creating your own Invoice template and add in your logo or plug in a personalized "thank you" note.

Clear Due Dates

Set deadlines for Tasks and specify the date and time something is due so everyone's working off the same deadline - no matter their time zone.

Time Plan for Personal and Business life

Every user can privately sync their Personal calendar with each Project Calendar (Calendar image of personal chores commitments fitting in around Project commitments).

Toggle Views

Switch between Daily, Weekly. Monthly and Agenda views (GIF: Project Calendar and click on Day, Week, Month and Agenda)

Time Tracking

You can record time spent on Tasks, send it to your budgets, prevent undercharging clients and get paid on time.

Birds Eye View

Oversee your remote team members work and provide clarity and context in one place. Share key resources, status updates, handle feedback requests and keep work on track in a remote work world.

Changes in the Workplace

With the after affects of covid 19 many people realised they can work more from home. Project 25 is the ideal tool to capitalise on this to save you money, keep your team happy and keep your business going forward.

Balance Workloads

Get a real-time overview of how busy team members are without having to check in with everyone individually. Keep your teams productive by monitoring and adjusting their workloads to ensure no one is overloaded and keep Projects on track.

Remote Working Side Benefits

End the commute stress, save time, fewer interruptions, less office politics and less (or more efficient) meetings all leads to increased productivity - a huge benefit for both employees and employers alike.
